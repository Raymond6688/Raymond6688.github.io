Gianluigi Buffon Ufficiale OMRI (born 28 January 1978) is an Italian professional footballer who captains and plays as a goalkeeper for the Serie B club Parma.

He is widely regarded as one of the greatest goalkeepers of all time.

He is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,100 professional career appearances.
